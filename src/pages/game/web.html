To start the game with Admiral Dashboard
To start your game and get critical information on your HQ and fleet statuses, you can open the Admiral Dashboard. This will show you information such as HQ Rank, Ship slots, Equipment LoS, Air Fighter Power, Fleet Speed, Morale, etc. You may activate this via:
  • For Windows / Linux: F12
  • or Ctrl + Shift + J
  • While on Mac: Cmd + Opt + I
When the Chrome panel opens, there will be a tab "KanColle"
To just start the game now
If you want to opt out of all those features and just want to start the game now without the "Admiral Dashboard", you may click the button below. Opening the panel too late in the middle of a gameplay session will not ensure that data is updated. Things like equipment list and construction timers are loaded at the start of the game.
